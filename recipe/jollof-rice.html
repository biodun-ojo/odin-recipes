description

Jollof rice is a delicious and flavorful one-pot rice dish popular in many West African countries

ingredent

  • rice: Long-grain rice is typically used.
  • Tomatoes: They are cooked down to form a base for the dish.
  • Onions: Chopped onions add depth of flavor.
  • Spices: Common spices include thyme, bay leaf, and paprika.

steps

  • Cook the base: Tomatoes, onions, and sometimes peppers are cooked down to form a thick sauce or paste.
  • Par-cook the rice: Rice may be rinsed and par-cooked in boiling water before adding it to the sauce.
  • Simmer: The rice and sauce are simmered together with spices and seasonings until the rice is cooked through and flavorful.
